🔭 ZEISS THROUGH TIME 🔭

🧪 HOW IT ALL STARTED

In 1846, Carl Zeiss opened a small workshop in Jena where he built precision optical instruments like microscopes. 【1-5bf9fe】

Soon, ZEISS became known for scientific innovation and high-quality optics!

🔬 SCIENCE POWER UP!

In the late 1800s, ZEISS teamed up with Ernst Abbe and Otto Schott to revolutionize optics using science and math 🤯 【2-15fa1c】

This enabled the first microscopes designed with precise calculations!

🌍 WORLD EVENTS & DRAMA

  • Survived World War I and II ⚠️
  • Company split into East & West Germany 😱
  • Reunited after German reunification 💪 【1-5bf9fe】

🌙 🚀 JOURNEY TO THE MOON 🚀 🌙

1960s SPACE RACE MODE ACTIVATED!!!

ZEISS developed special camera lenses for space missions used on NASA Apollo missions! 【3-9e35fc】

These lenses captured the famous photos of the Moon landing in 1969 😮 🌍

Over 30,000 images were taken during Apollo missions using ZEISS optics! 【3-9e35fc】

ZEISS lenses had to survive:

  • Zero gravity 🌌
  • Extreme temperatures 🔥❄️
  • Vacuum conditions 😬

Result: The most iconic images in human history 📸✨
